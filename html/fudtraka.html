TODAY'S RECIPE

Efo Riro

INGRIDIENTS

  • 1 Can of plum tomatoes
  • Red bell peppers (Tatashe)
  • 1-2 scotch bonnets
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 1/4 – 3/4 cup of chicken stock
  • Salt to taste
  • 1-2 chicken stock Maggi cubes
  • Assorted meat beef, chicken, pomo , dry fish
  • 2lbs of Red Spinach (Tete pupa)
  • 1/2 cup of dry crayfish
  • Palm oil
  • Iru (fermented Locust beans)

DIRECTIONS

  • In a blender, add 1-2 scotch bonnets, 1 red bell pepper, 1 large onion, and 1 can of tomatoes and blend together until thoroughly mixed.
  • In a large saucepan, add palm oil to cover the base. Once the oil is hot, add the iru then, pour in the blended tomato mixture and bring it to a boil. Add salt to taste, 1 maggi cube and stir. Let it cook on medium heat for 10-15 minutes
  • Then, add pre-cooked assorted meat (or any meat combination of your choice) and dry fish to the stew and let it cook for 10-15 more minutes.
  • Finally, add 4 bunches of spinach and lower the heat to a simmer. After 5 minutes add 1/2 a cup of shrimp and let it all cook for another 7-10 minutes. Serve and enjoy!
